Last updated: April 21, 2025 · Auburndale, Florida

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide to us, for example when you submit our contact form or reach out to us directly. This may include:

  • Your name and the name of your ministry or organization
  • Contact details such as email address and phone number

We do not sell products or process payments through this website, and we do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary to respond to your inquiry.

02 How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information. We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. 06 Your Rights

You may request to access, correct or delete the personal information we hold about you, or ask us to stop contacting you, at any time. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
