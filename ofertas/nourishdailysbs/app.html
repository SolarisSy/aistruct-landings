What it does

Simple, calm, genuinely useful

NourishTrack is built around noticing — not scoring, ranking, or shaming. Here's what you'll find inside.

💧

Water logging

Tap a cup to add water. Set a gentle reminder if you tend to forget during busy mornings.

🥗

Meal notes

Jot meals in your own words — "veggies, rice, beans" — no calorie counting required.

🚶

Movement notes

Log walks, stretching, gardening, anything that gets you moving. Everyday movement counts.

😴

Sleep notes

Record how rested you feel and roughly when you slept. Spot patterns over weeks, not days.

🔔

Friendly reminders

Choose one or two nudges a day — water after lunch, a stretch break. Fully customizable.

📊

Weekly snapshot

A calm weekly view of your habits. No grades, no streaks that punish you for missing a day.

How it works

Three quiet steps

You can be up and running in under a minute. No account quizzes, no onboarding gauntlet.

Pick a few habits

Choose water, meals, movement, and sleep — or start with just one. Add more whenever you like.

Log with a tap

One tap adds water. A quick line captures a meal. It takes seconds, not minutes.

Notice the patterns

Your weekly snapshot shows trends gently — like how you feel after better sleep or more water.

Adjust as you go

Life changes. Tweak reminders, add habits, or take a break. The app never nags you.

Mini FAQ

Quick questions, straight answers

Does NourishTrack count calories or tell me what to eat?

No. NourishTrack doesn't count calories, score meals, or recommend specific foods. It's a noticing tool — you write meals in your own words and spot patterns over time. For individualized nutrition guidance, talk with a registered dietitian or your healthcare provider.

Is it a weight-loss app?

No. NourishTrack is a general daily-habits and awareness app. We don't set weight goals, track body size, or promise any health outcome. It pairs well with the educational articles you'll find on Nourish Daily.

Will it lecture me if I miss a day?

Never. There are no streaks that reset, no red Xs, no guilt. You can take a day (or a month) off and your weekly snapshot simply waits for you to come back.

Does it work offline?

Yes. You can log and review habits without a connection. Data syncs to your own device account when you're online again. See our Privacy Policy for how data is handled.

Is NourishTrack free?

The core logging, reminders, and weekly snapshot are free to use. There's no advertising inside your logs. More questions? See our full FAQ page.
