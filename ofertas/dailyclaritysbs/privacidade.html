Privacy Policy

Daily Clarity publishes writing about attention, work routines and planning at dailyclarity.sbs. This page describes what we store and why.

What we collect

If you type an address into the newsletter box, it stays in your own browser — the form is a placeholder and nothing is transmitted to us. Beyond that we keep aggregate page counts (which article was opened, roughly how many times) with no name attached.

What we do not collect

No accounts, no payment details, no health information, no third-party advertising pixels. The pages load no external fonts, scripts or trackers; everything you see is served from this domain.

Cookies

We set no cookies of our own. Your browser may cache images and stylesheets, which you can clear at any time.

Your rights

Write to contact@dailyclarity.sbs and we will answer questions about anything stored, or remove it.

Editorial note

Articles here are general writing about work habits and planning. They are not professional, medical or financial advice for your particular situation.
