What it does

Small, well-made tools for a steadier day

MindClarity keeps things deliberately simple. It is designed to support everyday focus and reflection — never to diagnose, treat, or make promises about your mind.

🎯

Gentle focus blocks

Run a 25-minute deep work block, or build your own. Soft chimes mark transitions so your attention is not jolted.

📝

One-line journaling

Capture a single line of gratitude or a quick mood note. Low pressure, private to your device, easy to keep up.

📊

Calm weekly insights

A gentle summary of when your focus tended to feel steadiest — patterns to notice, not scores to chase.

🌙

Wind-down reminders

An optional nudge to step away from screens in the evening, in line with what we cover on sleep and recovery.

🔕

Distraction guard

During a focus block, MindClarity can quiet notifications from other apps so you can actually rest your attention.

🔒

Private by default

Your journal entries stay on your device. We do not sell personal data. See our Privacy Policy.

How it works

Three calm steps, repeated as often as you like

Choose a focus block

Pick a preset like "Deep work" or "Calm reading," or set your own length. The timer holds the structure so you do not have to.

Take a real break

When the chime sounds, step away for a few minutes. Genuine breaks are part of the method, not a reward you have to earn.

Notice your day

Add a one-line mood or gratitude note. Over a week, MindClarity gently reflects back when you tended to feel most settled.

Quick questions

About MindClarity

Is MindClarity a medical app?

No. MindClarity is a general wellness companion — a focus timer and journal. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition. If you are dealing with a health concern, please consult a qualified professional.

Will it improve my memory or IQ?

We make no such claims. MindClarity simply offers structure for focus and a place to reflect. Any benefits will vary from person to person and depend on many factors.

Where is my journal data stored?

Entries are kept locally on your device by default. We do not sell personal data. You can read the details in our Privacy Policy.

What if I miss a day?

Nothing happens. There are no punishing streaks. You can come and go as life allows — that is the whole point of a calm tool.

Is it free?

MindClarity offers a free tier with the core focus timer and journaling, plus an optional premium tier for extra themes and insights. Pricing details live inside the app.

How is this different from the blog?

The blog teaches the ideas; MindClarity is a tool to practice some of them — like structured focus and a moment of daily reflection.
